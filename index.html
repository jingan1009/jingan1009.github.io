Bio

I am a Phillip Griffiths Assistant Research Professor at Duke University. Previously, I was a postdoc researcher (July 2021- July 2022) at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences in Leipzig, Germany. I received my Ph.D. at Stanford University under the supervision of Lenya Ryzhik and Lexing Ying. Before that I was an undergraduate student at University of California, Los Angeles.

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in the mathematical aspects of machine learning and data science problems, using tools from stochastic analysis and partial differential equations. I also study evolution equations including reaction diffusion equations.
